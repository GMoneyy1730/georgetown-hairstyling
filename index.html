The Shop

Georgetown Hairstyling has been cutting hair on 35th Street since 1913. More than a century later, it's still the same kind of place: an honest, old-school barbershop a block from Georgetown University, where students, professors, and neighbors have been getting sharp for generations.

Walk in. Settle into the chair. Whether it's a classic men's cut, a women's shampoo and style, a beard trim, or a hot towel finish — the work here is done the way it's always been done. Carefully, without rushing, and at a price that won't surprise you.

The philosophy hasn't changed in over 110 years: do good work, treat people right, and earn them coming back. That's how a shop lasts a century in the same neighborhood.
